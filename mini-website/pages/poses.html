Basic Yoga Poses

Yoga begins with learning a few key poses that form the foundation of practice. Mountain Pose teaches proper alignment and grounding. Downward Dog stretches the spine and strengthens the shoulders. Child’s Pose offers rest whenever needed and is a gentle way to reconnect with your breath. Warrior poses build strength, confidence, and balance. Seated Forward Fold helps stretch tight hamstrings while calming the mind. At Beginner Yoga Hub, we demonstrate each pose step by step, ensuring you understand not only how to move, but also why each posture matters. Beginners are encouraged to use props like blocks or straps for added comfort and safety. These poses, practiced regularly, become second nature and prepare you for more advanced flows. Mastering the basics gives you a strong, safe foundation to build upon as your yoga journey unfolds.
