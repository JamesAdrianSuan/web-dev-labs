Pricing Options

At Beginner Yoga Hub, we believe that cost should never be a barrier to starting your yoga journey. We’ve designed flexible pricing plans that fit all budgets. A single drop-in class is a great way to test the waters before committing. For those ready to dive deeper, our monthly unlimited pass offers access to every class across all our studios. We also provide multi-class packs, perfect if you prefer to practice once or twice a week. Discounts are available for students, seniors, and first-time visitors, because inclusivity matters. Our memberships are straightforward, with no hidden fees or long contracts. By keeping yoga affordable, we help you maintain consistency, which is the key to progress. Investing in yoga is more than just paying for classes—it’s investing in your health, balance, and long-term well-being. Join today and find the plan that works best for your lifestyle.
