Our Locations

Beginner Yoga Hub is proud to serve the community with several accessible studios. Our main hub is centrally located, making it easy to reach by public transport. Bright, open, and welcoming, the main studio is perfect for newcomers who want a lively community atmosphere. For those seeking smaller group sizes, we also run neighborhood studios that provide more intimate classes. Every space is fully equipped with mats, blocks, straps, and blankets—so you never have to worry about bringing gear. We’ve carefully designed each location to feel calming, with soft lighting, natural elements, and plenty of space to breathe. And if travel isn’t possible, our online livestreams let you join from the comfort of your home. Whether you’re in the city center or a nearby neighborhood, there’s always a Beginner Yoga Hub class within reach. Your journey can begin wherever you are.
